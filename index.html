🌍 Why SkillSeed?

Millions of students in India pass through school without clarity on careers, soft skills, or real-world readiness. We fill that gap with structured, inspiring, and activity-based programs aligned with NEP 2020.

📚 Our Programs

Career Awareness

Discover a wide range of career paths through real-life stories, roadmaps, and mentor talks.

Life Skills & Decision Making

Boost emotional intelligence, resilience, and practical thinking for everyday life.

Communication Skills

From speaking confidently to presenting ideas — learn to express yourself clearly and effectively.

AI & Coding Basics (Coming Soon)

Introduction to future tech — build fun, hands-on projects in AI, block coding, and more.

Financial Literacy

Learn how to manage money, budget smartly, and understand digital payments — skills every teen should have.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Ignite your entrepreneurial spirit with creative problem-solving and innovation workshops.

🌱 Our Mission

To empower every student from underserved backgrounds with future-ready skills, life confidence, and career clarity – aligned with NEP 2020.

🚀 Our Vision

To be India's most trusted life-skills learning partner for students from Classes 6 to 10.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What age group are SkillSeed programs for?

We currently cater to students from classes 6 to 12, tailoring content for each stage of growth.

Are sessions online or offline?

We offer both formats depending on school partnership and student needs. Hybrid is also possible!

How can schools collaborate?

Schools can reach out to us via this contact form. We’ll schedule a call and customize the program based on needs.

Can individuals register directly?

Yes! Individual students and parents can also register for our weekend programs or summer bootcamps.
